Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Product Commitment

AngryMan is a personal safety app designed for women living alone. Privacy is a core principle of our design.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data.

  • No account information, contacts, location, call logs, or unique device identifiers are collected.
  • No recordings, transformed voice outputs, role settings, or scenario settings are uploaded.
  • No third-party analytics, ad SDKs, or behavior tracking services are integrated.

Your voice input, AI transformation, call simulation, and settings are processed locally on your device.

3. Local Permissions and Storage

  • The app requests permissions only when needed (for example, microphone access).
  • Temporary local cache is used only to improve in-app performance and can be cleared by the user.
  • No local content is synchronized to any cloud service.

4. Sharing and Cross-Border Transfer

4.1 Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent user data to third parties because we do not collect user data.

4.2 Cross-Border Transfer

Because user data is not collected or stored on cloud servers, cross-border transfer does not apply.

5. Policy Updates

If privacy-related behavior changes in future versions, this page will be updated before those changes are released.
